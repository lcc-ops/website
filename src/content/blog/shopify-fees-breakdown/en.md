Shopify's headline number is "$39/month." The real cost is much higher. Here's every fee a typical store pays in 2026.

## Subscription tiers

| Plan | Monthly | Best for |
|---|---|---|
| Starter | $5 | Side projects, in-person sales |
| Basic | $39 | New stores, < $10k/month revenue |
| Shopify | $105 | Growing stores, more reports |
| Advanced | $399 | $1M+/year, lowest card rates |

The 3-year plan discounts Basic to $29/month and Advanced to $299/month.

## Transaction fees — Shopify Payments

If you use Shopify Payments (powered by Stripe):

| Channel | Online | In-person |
|---|---|---|
| Basic plan | 2.9% + $0.30 | 2.7% + $0.00 |
| Shopify plan | 2.7% + $0.30 | 2.5% + $0.00 |
| Advanced plan | 2.5% + $0.30 | 2.4% + $0.00 |

For international cards (non-US), add **1.5%** to the percentage.

## Transaction fees — third-party gateways

If you use PayPal, Stripe directly, or another gateway:

- Basic plan: **2.0%** extra on top of what the gateway charges
- Shopify plan: 1.0%
- Advanced plan: 0.5%

The math: Basic + PayPal = 3.49% + $0.30 + PayPal's fee. Stick with Shopify Payments until you process $10k+/month.

## What a $50 sale actually nets

Assume Shopify Basic + Shopify Payments, no apps charging transaction fees:

```
Gross revenue:           $50.00
Shopify Payments fee:    −$1.75  (2.9% + $0.30 = $1.45 + $0.30)
Subscription allocation: −$0.65  ($39 ÷ 60 sales/month)
                          ──────
Net before COGS:         $47.60
```

That's 4.8% platform cost on a $50 sale. At $20 sale it's 7.4%. At $200 it's 3.6%.

## Hidden fees most sellers forget

### 1. Currency conversion

Shopify Payments charges **1.5% extra** for non-USD transactions and another 1–2% inside the FX spread. Sell in USD whenever possible.

### 2. Refund fees

Shopify keeps the transaction fee on refunds. You don't get it back. This hits apparel stores hard — a 15% return rate means you paid $1.75 in fees to process the sale, then refunded the customer and kept paying that fee for nothing.

### 3. Chargeback fees

$15 per chargeback, regardless of outcome. Even if you win, you paid $15 to defend yourself.

### 4. App transaction fees

Some apps (subscriptions, wholesale, marketplaces) charge **2% on top** of Shopify Payments. Check your app stack.

### 5. Theme costs

Free themes are limited. Most serious stores pay $150–350 once for a paid theme. Avoid "free theme" marketplaces with hidden license terms.

### 6. Email costs

Shopify Email is free for the first 10,000 emails/month, then $0.001/email. Klaviyo integration starts at $45/month after 500 profiles.

## The full monthly bill for a real store

| Item | Cost |
|---|---|
| Basic subscription | $39 |
| Paid theme (amortized) | $12 |
| 5 essential apps (avg) | $90 |
| Email tool (1k contacts) | $30 |
| Domain | $1 |
| **Total fixed** | **$172/month** |
| Transaction fees (on $10k revenue) | ~$320 |
| **All-in platform cost** | **$492** |

That's **4.9%** of revenue. Bake it into your pricing.

## When to upgrade plans

Stay on Basic until:

- You process $10k/month → Shopify plan pays for itself in lower transaction fees
- You need third-party calculated shipping rates
- You want gift cards

Move to Advanced at $1M+/year when the lower rates and better reports pay for the $399/month.

## Free alternatives for the first $5k/month

- **Stripe + free landing page** — under $50/month in fees
- **Gumroad** — 10% flat, no monthly
- **Payhip** — 5% + $0 fixed, no monthly

These work while you're validating. Switch to Shopify when you need inventory, shipping labels, or staff accounts.
