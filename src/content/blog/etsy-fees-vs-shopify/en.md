Etsy and Shopify look like the same job — sell handmade goods — but their fee structures diverge at every line. Here is the real cost on a $35 product, plus the breakeven volume where each wins.

## Fee stack on a $35 product

| Fee | Etsy | Shopify Basic + Stripe |
|---|---|---|
| Subscription | $0 | $39/month |
| Listing / product page | $0.20 | $0 |
| Transaction fee | 6.5% = $2.28 | 0% |
| Payment processing | 3% + $0.25 = $1.30 | 2.9% + $0.30 = $1.32 |
| **Platform + payment total** | **$3.78** | **$1.32** |
| Offsite ads (15% on attributed sales) | $5.25 (if attributed) | $0 |
| **Total with offsite ads** | **$9.03** | **$1.32** |
| Shipping label discount | 10–30% off USPS | Variable |

Etsy is cheaper per order for low-volume sellers, but offsite ads and the $0.20 listing can flip the math fast.

## Listing fees in detail

Every Etsy listing costs $0.20. Listings auto-renew every 4 months, or when sold.

- 100 active listings, 4 months no sales: $20 in renewal fees
- 100 active listings, all sold in 4 months: $0 (renewed on sale)

For sellers with high SKU counts and slow turnover, listings add up. A 1,000-SKU shop costs $200 every 4 months just to stay visible.

## Transaction + payment fee math

| Order value | Etsy (6.5% + 3% + $0.25) | Shopify Basic + Stripe (2.9% + $0.30) |
|---|---|---|
| $10 | $1.43 | $0.59 |
| $20 | $2.23 | $0.88 |
| $35 | $3.78 | $1.32 |
| $50 | $5.00 | $1.75 |
| $100 | $8.75 | $3.20 |

Etsy takes roughly 3× the platform+payment cut at every price point.

## Offsite ads

Etsy runs paid ads off its platform (Google, Facebook, Pinterest). Sellers under $10,000 annual revenue pay 15% on attributed sales. Above $10K, it drops to 12%.

You cannot opt out until you cross $10K. For sellers in that 0–$10K tier, offsite ads can flip Etsy from "cheaper" to "much more expensive."

A 30% offsite attribution rate on a $35 product adds $5.25 per attributed order. For a seller with 100 orders/month and 30% attribution, that is $157.50/month — already 4× the Shopify subscription.

## Shipping label discounts

Etsy offers USPS, FedEx and Canada Post labels at a 10–30% discount. For a $5 USPS Priority label, expect $3.50–$4.50 instead of full retail.

Shopify Shipping offers comparable rates on USPS, UPS, DHL, and others. The label cost is similar between platforms; the difference is the platform fee on top.

## Breakeven: Etsy vs Shopify

For a $35 product with no offsite ads:

```
cost_etsy = 0.20 + 0.065 × 35 + 0.03 × 35 + 0.25 = $3.78
cost_shopify_per_order = 39 / n + 0.029 × 35 + 0.30 = 39/n + 1.32
```

Breakeven when 3.78 = 39/n + 1.32, or n = 39 / 2.46 = ~16 orders/month.

Below 16 orders/month, Etsy is cheaper on a pure fee basis. Above that, Shopify wins.

But once offsite ads come in:

```
cost_etsy_with_ads = 3.78 + 0.15 × 35 = $9.03
```

Breakeven moves to: 9.03 = 39/n + 1.32, or n = 5 orders/month. Above 5 orders/month, Shopify is cheaper even with offsite ads.

## When to use which

**Etsy wins when:**

- You sell fewer than 5–10 orders/month
- Your products are discovery-driven (vintage, niche handmade)
- You are starting and want free infrastructure
- You sell below $20 AOV (no offsite ad impact at this price)

**Shopify wins when:**

- You exceed ~10 orders/month
- You want email lists, custom domains, abandoned-cart recovery
- You can pay for your own ads
- You want a brand that survives a platform change

**Both at once:** Many sellers use Shopify for their own brand and Etsy for discovery. Buy on Etsy, redirect to Shopify for repeat purchases. Cost: 1.5× the fees, gain: owned customer list.

## Hidden costs on both

| | Etsy | Shopify |
|---|---|---|
| Returns | Most categories: seller pays return shipping | Seller-paid unless using a returns app |
| Currency conversion | 1.5% on non-USD sales | 2% (Shopify Payments multi-currency) |
| Dispute fee | $15 per chargeback | $15 per chargeback |
| App / tool stack | $0–$30/month (Etsy-specific tools) | $50–$200/month (typical stack) |

Shopify's app stack is the hidden trap. Most stores spend $100–$200/month on apps before they realize it.
